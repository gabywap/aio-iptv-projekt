Skopiuj poniższe komendy i wklej je w terminalu tunera:

wget -q "--no-check-certificate" https://raw.githubusercontent.com/OliOli2013/PanelAIO-Plugin/main/installer.sh -O - | /bin/sh
# IPTV Dream (Listy IPTV):
wget -q "--no-check-certificate" https://raw.githubusercontent.com/OliOli2013/IPTV-Dream-Plugin/main/installer.sh -O - | /bin/sh
